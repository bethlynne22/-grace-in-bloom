Grace in Bloom: Genesis 1

“In the beginning, God created the heavens and the earth.” — Genesis 1:1

📖 Read Genesis 1

Public-domain summary path. Later we can add full WEB/KJV text.

🍯 Study & Honey Drops

Main Honey Drop

Before anything else begins, God is already present, powerful, and good.

Big Idea

Genesis 1 teaches that God is Creator. He speaks, orders, fills, blesses, and calls creation good. Humanity is made in God's image and given purpose.

Hebrew Word Study

בְּרֵאשִׁית — Bereshit (beh-ray-SHEET): “In the beginning.”

טוֹב — Tov (tohv): good, pleasing, beautiful, right.

צֶלֶם — Tzelem (TSEH-lem): image. Genesis 1 says humanity is made in the image of God.

Application

  • Where do I need God to bring light?
  • How can I honor God's image in myself and others?
  • What part of creation makes me worship?
